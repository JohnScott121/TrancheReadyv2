Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to integrate my systems?
No. CSV in → ZIP out. No credentials, no API access.
What’s in the evidence pack?
Clients, Transactions, Cases, Program.html, and a manifest with SHA-256 hashes (and optional Ed25519 signature).
Is AI required?
No. Narratives are optional if a key is present; exports never block.
How is data protected?
Memory uploads, strict headers, rate limiting, and short-lived verify links.
Which corridors are monitored?
RU, CN, HK, AE, IN, IR — with at least one transfer ≥ A$20k and ≥2 total.
